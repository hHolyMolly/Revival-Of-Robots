About us

The game features an opportunity to trade and earn, to collect resources for developing your character, to build settlements from a simple village to megacities, to equip your hero and to take part in thrilling battles with other players in a post-apocalyptic setting.

Game

Revival of Robots isn't just a decentralized collectible GameFi ecosystem, it's an entire post-apocalyptic Roboverse! You're in for a thoroughly developed story, gameplay, and game mechanics.

Mission

This project targets the most important issues of our day and age, encouraging a new perspective on the rapid development of technology and the impact that humans have been making on the environment.

To ensure that the Revival oj Robots story stays a work of fiction in our world, we plan to support blockchain-based eco-projects by buying these projects' tokens and distributing them among members of our DAO.

We want to establish an engaged and motivated community that will support our ideas, organize themselves in a DAO, and be as interested as we are in a better future for our planet!

Economy

Rayz

All of the technology in the Revival of Robots game is based on a unique energy source called Rayzen. Rayzen can be harvested from mines located all over the planet. Mankind has nearly exhausted it all. So it pays to be smart about how you use this expensive energy for your needs.

You can use RAYZ tokens to buy lands, combat Robomechs, personalize your robot avatar, get the original landscape skin, buy different items to customize your lands, and even change the weather in the region.

Main Elements

The economy depends on four main elements: bolt, screws, wood, and water. You can mix and match them in various combinations to create new and improved in-game items (from simple pickaxes or hatchets to high-tech systems).

When a player correctly guesses a crafting recipe, they receive:

• a new item for further research;
• a limited-edition NFT prize token.

Your mission is to create the items needed to craft a building from basic elements. The owners of this building will be able to purchase lands for the RAYZ tokens they earn to then start conquering the Revival of Robots Roboverse!

Land Sale

Buy land, rent it out, travel, play mini-games, change landscapes and climate — all for RAYZ.

Mining Tools

Mine the RAYZ game token on an industrial scale with the help of mining tools.

Staking Nft

Each of the unique NFT collectible cards greatly improves token mining by stacking.

All the cards look different and vary in rarity.

The player receives a pack with NFT.

Prize

For additional prizes, become (one of) the first to discover a new element. You get:

  • A % of the prize pool paid in Blockchain tokens;
  • A Mythical NFT for discovering a technoline;
  • A Legendary NFT given to 3 Discoverers;
  • A Discoverer's name immortalized on an NFT item;
  • Upgrade to the next step in game rankings and get a new rank.
